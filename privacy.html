Smuggle Privacy Policy

The latest version of the Smuggle Privacy Policy is always available here: http://vikingco.de/Smuggle/privacy.html

Information Collected

Your IP addresses is recorded and logged everywhere on the Internet. Smuggle stores credentials of the logged in user only and strictly for the use within the application. Smuggle does not sell nor trade these logs.

Sharing Data

Smuggle allows you to share data with third party applications such as Mail or social networks. Such information sharing is at your own discretion and will always involve your approval. Your use of any third party applications or services is governed by those applications/services individual privacy policies.
